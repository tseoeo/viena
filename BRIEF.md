Should include logistics (transport, walking distances, where to sit and rest, café stops), accessibility notes for each major site, and what's open / closed on **May 1 (Tag der Arbeit)**.

## Scope and Constraints
- Stay realistic about pacing. Two palaces in a day is too much. One palace + its gardens + a café is a full day.
- Prefer sights with elevators, benches, garden cafés, and short queues (or skip-the-line tickets worth pre-booking).
- Account for Friday 1 May being a public holiday in Austria — note any closures, transport changes, or May Day demonstrations on the Ringstraße that might affect movement.
- Hotel is in the 3rd district. **Belvedere is a 10–15 minute walk** from the hotel — this is a major planning fact, weight it heavily. Stadtpark and the Musikverein are also walkable / one tram stop.
- Schönbrunn is across town and a half-day commitment; assess whether it's worth it given the mobility constraint, or whether Belvedere + Hofburg is the better "imperial" pairing.
- Sunday is a partial day — light morning before checkout / departure to airport or station.
